About Harshad Shantilal Mehta:

He was an Indian stockbroker and businessman.

Mehta worked in various sales-related jobs. He started as a salesperson at NIACL, where he developed an interest in the stock market. He eventually joined Harjivandas Nemidas Securities and was mentored by Prasann Pranjivandas.

By 1990, he was known as the “Amitabh Bachchan of the Stock Market”, a major influencer in the Indian securities world.

He founded Grow More Research and Asset Management, traded heavily in ACC shares, and introduced the "replacement cost theory" to justify stock valuation.

In 1990–91, he gained the title "The Big Bull" and was admired for his luxurious lifestyle and media popularity.

Major Achievements

  • Richest Stock Broker in India during his time
  • Involvement in the 1992 Securities Scam worth ₹30,000 crore
  • Gained infamy for market manipulation
  • Known as “Amitabh Bachchan of the stock market”
